Olga moved to Palm Beach County from Camaguey, Cuba at the age of 3. She graduated from Forest Hill High School and began working at the Palm Beach County Courthouse as a court clerk. Passionate about law, she became a probation officer for Pride Probation and shortly thereafter she got her first opportunity to serve process. With the encouragement and support of her family and friends, and two daughters to care for - she took a leap to launch Signed Sealed & Delivered and here we are now, nearly 30 years later! As the President and founder of SSD, Olga has the pleasure of employing individuals whom she not only works with daily, but treats as family. Every one of SSD's employees have had a longstanding relationship with the company. Olga is passionate about helping others.
