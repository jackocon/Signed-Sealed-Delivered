Since established in 1988, Signed, Sealed & Delivered, Inc. has offered professional, efficient and prompt service of process. We provide service throughout the state of Florida and have a Nationwide network, as well as Canada, Bahamas and Puerto Rico. All of our servers are licensed and bonded, and many are bi-lingual in spanish. Status is updated daily, and e-mailed directly to you. Our clients are provided with a user name and password to be used on our Check Status page. Here, you can view comments, details about the serve, and print affidavits and invoices.
